1669: Mow shrubland to reduce impacts of pollutants
Effectiveness rating: Unknown effectiveness
Key Messages:

One randomized, replicated, controlled study in the UK found that mowing to reduce the impact of nitrogen deposition did not alter shoot length of common heather or the number of purple moor grass seedlings.
One controlled study in the UK found that mowing a heathland affected by nitrogen pollution did not alter the cover or shoot length of heather compared to areas where prescribed burning was used.
