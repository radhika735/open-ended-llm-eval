1457: Install rope or pole (canopy) bridges
Effectiveness rating: Likely to be beneficial
Key Messages:

One study in Brazil found that black lion tamarins and capuchins used a pole bridge to cross a road.
One before-and-after study in Belize found that a black howler monkey population increased after the construction of pole bridges over man-made gaps.
One before-and-after study in Madagascar found that all six monitored lemur species used bridges to cross roads and pipelines.
