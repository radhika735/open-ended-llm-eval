3599: Install exclusion devices on fishing gear: Snakes & lizards
Effectiveness rating: Awaiting assessment
Key Messages:

One study evaluated the effects of installing exclusion devices on fishing gear on snake and lizard populations. This study was in Australia.

Unwanted catch (1 study): One replicated study off the coast of Western Australia found that exclusion grids did not prevent sea snakes from entering trawl nets.
