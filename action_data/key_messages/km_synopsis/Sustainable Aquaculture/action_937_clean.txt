937: Eco friendly biofouling prevention
Effectiveness rating: Awaiting assessment
Key Messages:

A replicated, controlled study in Australia found silicon coated nets accumulated less biofouling organisms which could be more easily removed than untreated nets. 
Two replicated, controlled trials found that a temperature of 50oC prevented the settlement of actinulae and hydroids on nets
