3398: Sow grassland forb species
Effectiveness rating: Evidence not assessed
Key Messages:

One study examined the effects of sowing grassland forb species on grassland vegetation. The study was in Belgium.

VEGETATION COMMUNITY (0 STUDIES)
VEGETATION ABUNDANCE (0 STUDIES)
VEGETATION STRUCTURE (0 STUDIES)
OTHER (1 STUDY)

Germination/Emergence (1 study): One replicated, controlled study in Belgium found that sowing seeds of grassland forb species did not increase the number of seedlings for three forb species.
