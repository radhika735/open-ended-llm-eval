2345: Issue enforcement notices to deter use of non bear-proof garbage dumpsters to reduce human-wildlife conflict
Effectiveness rating: Unknown effectiveness
Key Messages:

One study evaluated the effects of issuing enforcement notices to deter use of non bear-proof garbage dumpsters to reduce human-wildlife conflict. This study was in the USA.

Human-wildlife conflict (1 study): A replicated, controlled, before-and-after study in the USA found that issuing enforcement notices requiring appropriate dumpster use did not reduce garbage accessibility to black bears.
