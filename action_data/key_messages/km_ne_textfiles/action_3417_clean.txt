3417: Burn vegetation before seeding/planting
Effectiveness rating: No evidence found (no assessment)
Key Messages:

We found no studies that evaluated the effects of burning vegetation before seeding/planting on grassland vegetation.

Therefore we have been unable to assess whether or not the action is effective or has any harmful impacts. Please get in touch if you know of such a study for this action.
