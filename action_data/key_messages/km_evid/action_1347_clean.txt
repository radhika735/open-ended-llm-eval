1347: Crop production: Add manure to the soil
Effectiveness rating: Unknown effectiveness
Key Messages:
Crop yield (2 studies): One replicated, randomized, controlled study from Greece found higher maize yields in plots with added manure, compared to plots without added manure, in two of three comparisons. One replicated, randomized, controlled study from Italy found similar nectarine yields in plots with or without added manure.
