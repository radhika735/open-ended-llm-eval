1585: Parrot’s feather: Water level drawdown
Effectiveness rating: Unknown effectiveness
Key Messages:

One replicated, randomized, controlled laboratory study in the USA found that water removal to expose plants to drying during the summer led to lower survival of parrot’s feather plants than exposing plants to drying during the winter.
