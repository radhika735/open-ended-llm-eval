608: Artificially incubate and hand-rear rails in captivity
Effectiveness rating: Unknown effectiveness
Key Messages:
A controlled before-and-after study from New Zealand found that post-release survival of hand-reared takahe Porphyrio hochstetteri (formerly P. mantelli) was as high as wild-reared birds and that six of ten released females raised chicks.
