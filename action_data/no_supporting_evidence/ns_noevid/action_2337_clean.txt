2337: Provide paths to limit extent of disturbance to mammals
Effectiveness rating: No evidence found (no assessment)
Background information and definitions:
In open habitats that are popular with human visitors for recreation, providing paths for people to use may reduce the overall area of ground on which mammals are vulnerable to human disturbance.

Key Messages:

We found no studies that evaluated the effects on mammals of providing paths to limit the extent of disturbance to mammals.

‘We found no studies’ means that we have not yet found any studies that have directly evaluated this intervention during our systematic journal and report searches. Therefore, we have no evidence to indicate whether or not the intervention has any desirable or harmful effects.
