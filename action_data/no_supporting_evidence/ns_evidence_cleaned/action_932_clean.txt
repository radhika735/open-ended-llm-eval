932: Integrated aquaculture systems
Effectiveness rating: Awaiting assessment
Background information and definitions:


Key Messages:

One replicated, controlled study in the USA reported greater growth of scallops grown next to fish farms.
A study in Canada found that blue mussels absorbed waste from a salmon farm.
A replicated, controlled study in Scotland reported enhanced algal growth when placed adjacent to fish farms. 
One study in Spain found no evidence that oyster and mussels were feeding on fish farm waste.
