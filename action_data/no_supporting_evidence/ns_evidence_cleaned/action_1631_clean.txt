1631: Cut trees and remove leaf litter
Effectiveness rating: Unknown effectiveness
Background information and definitions:
This section considers the cutting of trees combined with removal of leaf litter. The cutting of trees may reduce their abundance while the removal of leaf litter may aid the propagation of shrubland plant species.
