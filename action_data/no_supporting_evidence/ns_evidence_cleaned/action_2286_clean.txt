2286: Restore bat foraging habitat at ex-quarry sites
Effectiveness rating: Unknown effectiveness
Background information and definitions:
Abandoned mining sites, such as quarries, may be rehabilitated to provide foraging habitat for bats, e.g. through the restoration of grassland, trees and wetlands.

Key Messages:

One study evaluated the effects of restoring bat foraging habitat at ex-quarry sites on bat populations. The study was in France.
