1714: Plant/seed under established vegetation
Effectiveness rating: Unknown effectiveness
Background information and definitions:
Planting or sowing seeds under established vegetation may expose focal plants to less extreme conditions than planting them in unvegetated areas and therefore may result in higher survival and growth.
