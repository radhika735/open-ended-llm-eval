2605: Site management for target mammal species carried out by field sport practitioners 
Effectiveness rating: Unknown effectiveness
Background information and definitions:
Hunters and field sport participants may manage sites specifically to maintaining populations of their target mammal species. Management could include predator control and management of habitat features.

Key Messages:

One study evaluated the effects of site management for a target mammal species being carried out by field sport practitioners. This study was in Ireland.
