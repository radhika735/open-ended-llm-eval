2842: Create designated areas or access points for recreational activities
Effectiveness rating: No evidence found (no assessment)
Background information and definitions:
Creating designated areas or access points (e.g. slip ways) for recreational activities that are located away from important marine and freshwater mammal habitats may reduce disturbance.

Key Messages:

We found no studies that evaluated the effects of creating designated areas or access points for recreational activities on marine and freshwater mammal populations.

‘We found no studies’ means that we have not yet found any studies that have directly evaluated this action during our systematic journal and report searches. Therefore we have been unable to assess whether or not the action is effective or has any harmful impacts. Please get in touch if you know of such a study for this action.
