1319: Primates: Present food which requires the use (or modification) of tools
Effectiveness rating: No evidence found (no assessment)
Background information and definitions:
To be classified as a tool, an object must be held in the hand, foot or mouth by the primate and used to enable him or her to attain an immediate goal (Goodall 1980), such as acquiring food.
Goodall J (1980) Tool-using in primates and other vertebrates. Advances in the study of behaviour. New York, Academic Press, 1980, Vol. 3, 195-429.
 

Key Messages:

No evidence was captured for the effects of presenting food to primates that required the use (or modification) of tools.
