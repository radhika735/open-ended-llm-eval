        "background_definitions": "<p>The addition of fertilizer includes addition of any substance to the soil that may increase its fertility. This includes addition of chemical fertilizers, as well as compost and other organic matter. Addition of fertilizers to soils can help to increase nutrient concentration. Addition of organic matter can also increase soil microbe abundance in degraded soils (Ros <em>et al</em><em>.</em> 2003). These changes in soil conditions may increase the likelihood of colonisation of a degraded habitat by grassland species, particularly when combined with sowing seeds.</p>\r\n<p>The studies detailed in this intervention are direct tests of the effectiveness of adding fertilizer to soil before or after seeding or planting (e.g. by comparison with an unfertilized but seeded or planted plot).
