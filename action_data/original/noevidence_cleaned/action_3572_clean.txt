3572: Cease or prohibit discharge of waste effluents overboard from vessels
Effectiveness rating: No evidence found (no assessment)
Background information and definitions:
Discharge of waste effluents from vessels can contain pollutants. Reducing the amount of waste discharged from vessels may be beneficial for aquatic and semi-aquatic reptiles.

Key Messages:

We found no studies that evaluated the effects on reptile populations of ceasing or prohibiting discharge of waste effluents overboard from vessels.

‘We found no studies’ means that we have not yet found any studies that have directly evaluated this action during our systematic journal and report searches. Therefore we have been unable to assess whether or not the action is effective or has any harmful impacts. Please get in touch if you know of such a study for this action.
