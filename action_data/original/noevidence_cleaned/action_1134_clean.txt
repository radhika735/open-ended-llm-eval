1134: Floating pennywort: Public education
Effectiveness rating: No evidence found (no assessment)
Background information and definitions:
Once escaped in the wild, fragmentation of floating pennywort Hydrocotyle ranunculoides and resultant dispersal can result from a variety of management and recreational activities.  Direct sales of floating pennywort are now less common in the UK due to regulatory restrictions, although labelling can be unreliable.
 

Key Messages:

No evidence was captured on the impact of education programmes on control of floating pennywort.
