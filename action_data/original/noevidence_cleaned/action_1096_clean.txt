1096: Ponto-Caspian gammarids: Cleaning equipment
Effectiveness rating: No evidence found (no assessment)
Background information and definitions:
Cleaning sampling equipment and fishing gear using high pressure water sprays may help to reduce the risk of transporting gammarid shrimps between water bodies.
 

Key Messages:

No evidence was captured for the cleaning of equipment to control Ponto-Caspian gammarids.
