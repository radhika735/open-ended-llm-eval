2785: Introduce alternative income sources to reduce marine and freshwater mammal exploitation and trade
Effectiveness rating: No evidence found (no assessment)
Background information and definitions:
Introducing alternative income sources to replace the trade of marine and freshwater mammals may reduce hunting pressure. This could include cultivating agricultural products or rearing domestic animals. Eco-tourism may also provide an alternative source of income, although careful implementation may be required.

Key Messages:

We found no studies that evaluated the effects of introducing alternative income sources to reduce marine and freshwater mammal exploitation and trade.

‘We found no studies’ means that we have not yet found any studies that have directly evaluated this action during our systematic journal and report searches. Therefore we have been unable to assess whether or not the action is effective or has any harmful impacts. Please get in touch if you know of such a study for this action.
