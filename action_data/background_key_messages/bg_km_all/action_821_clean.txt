821: Reduce competition from native amphibians
Effectiveness rating: Unknown effectiveness
Background information and definitions:
Management for threatened amphibian species can sometimes include reducing numbers of a common amphibian species that compete for resources.
 

Key Messages:

One replicated, site comparison study in the UK found that natterjack toad populations did not increase following common toad control.
