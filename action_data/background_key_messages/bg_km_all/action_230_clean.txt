230: Maintain upland heath/moor
Effectiveness rating: Unknown effectiveness
Background information and definitions:
Unpland heath and moorland is maintained through unenclosed upland grazing. This intervention includes grazing on acid grassland, dry and wet upland heath.
 

Key Messages:
A literature review from the UK found that agri-environment guidelines on moorland grazing were leading to increased bird populations in one region. There were localised problems with overgrazing, burning and scrub encroachment.
