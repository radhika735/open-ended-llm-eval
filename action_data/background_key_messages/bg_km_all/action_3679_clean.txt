3679: Engage policy makers to make policy changes beneficial to reptiles
Effectiveness rating: No evidence found (no assessment)
Background information and definitions:
Engaging with and raising awareness amongst policymakers (e.g. CITES management and scientific authorities) about specific threats to reptiles, and the need for conservation, may result in improved legal protection of reptiles and their habitats.

Key Messages:

We found no studies that evaluated the effects on reptile populations of engaging policy makers to make policy changes beneficial to reptiles.

‘We found no studies’ means that we have not yet found any studies that have directly evaluated this action during our systematic journal and report searches. Therefore we have been unable to assess whether or not the action is effective or has any harmful impacts. Please get in touch if you know of such a study for this action.
