387: Control predators not on islands for gamebirds
Effectiveness rating: Awaiting assessment
Background information and definitions:


Key Messages:

Four controlled studies in Europe found increased populations or productivity of grouse and partridges on sites with predator removal. One study tested multiple interventions simultaneously.
A fifth replicated UK study found no increase in grouse densities or reproductive success on sites with gamekeepers, compared to those without.
