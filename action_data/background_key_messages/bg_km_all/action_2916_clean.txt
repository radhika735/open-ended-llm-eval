2916: Enforce existing legislation for habitat protection
Effectiveness rating: No evidence found (no assessment)
Background information and definitions:
Despite the legal protection of habitats or areas for marine and freshwater mammals, prohibited human activities may still occur. This intervention involves enforcing existing legislation for habitat protection to reduce illegal activities. This may involve surveillance, policing, and prosecution of offenders.

Key Messages:

We found no studies that evaluated the effects of enforcing existing legislation for habitat protection on marine and freshwater mammal populations.

‘We found no studies’ means that we have not yet found any studies that have directly evaluated this action during our systematic journal and report searches. Therefore we have been unable to assess whether or not the action is effective or has any harmful impacts. Please get in touch if you know of such a study for this action.
