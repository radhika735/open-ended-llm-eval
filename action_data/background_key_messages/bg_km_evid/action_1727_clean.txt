1727: Disturb vegetation
Effectiveness rating: Unknown effectiveness
Background information and definitions:
Disturbing vegetation reduces ground cover, allowing more light to reach the ground, and potentially increasing the germination of shrubland species.

Key Messages:

One randomized, replicated, controlled study in the UKÂ found that vegetation disturbance did not increase the abundance or species richness of specialist plants but increased the abundance of generalist plants.
