3560: Introduce legislation to control the use of hazardous substances
Effectiveness rating: No evidence found (no assessment)
Background information and definitions:
Introducing legislation to control the use of hazardous substances across a range of sectors (e.g. agriculture, manufacturing, energy production) could reduce the negative impacts on wildlife, including reptiles. Such laws exist in some countries.

Key Messages:

We found no studies that evaluated the effects on reptile populations of introducing legislation to control the use of hazardous substances.

‘We found no studies’ means that we have not yet found any studies that have directly evaluated this action during our systematic journal and report searches. Therefore we have been unable to assess whether or not the action is effective or has any harmful impacts. Please get in touch if you know of such a study for this action.
