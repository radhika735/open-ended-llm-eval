194: Mark nests during harvest 
Effectiveness rating: Likely to be ineffective or harmful
Background information and definitions:
Marking the nests of ground-nesting birds may reduce the accidental destruction by farmers during harvest or mowing.
 

Key Messages:
A replicated study from the Netherlands found that northern lapwing Vanellus vanellus nests were less likely to be destroyed when they were marked, compared to when they were not.
