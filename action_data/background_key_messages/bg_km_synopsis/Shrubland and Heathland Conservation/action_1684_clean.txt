1684: Remove trees, leaf litter and topsoil
Effectiveness rating: No evidence found (no assessment)
Background information and definitions:
Removing trees allows more light to reach the ground, potentially increasing the germination of shrubland species. Removing topsoil and leaf litter in addition to this may further increase the probability of seed germination.

Key Messages:

We found no studies that evaluated the effects of removing trees, leaf litter and soil surface on shrublands.
