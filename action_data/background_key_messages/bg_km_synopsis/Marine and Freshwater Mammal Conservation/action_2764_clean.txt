2764: Limit vessel traffic in shallow rivers
Effectiveness rating: No evidence found (no assessment)
Background information and definitions:
This intervention involves limiting vessel traffic within shallow rivers where freshwater mammals, such as river dolphins, are likely to be particularly vulnerable to disturbance and at greater risk of being involved in collisions.
See also Reduce shipping along inland waterways.

Key Messages:

We found no studies that evaluated the effects of limiting vessel traffic in shallow rivers on freshwater mammal populations.

‘We found no studies’ means that we have not yet found any studies that have directly evaluated this action during our systematic journal and report searches. Therefore we have been unable to assess whether or not the action is effective or has any harmful impacts. Please get in touch if you know of such a study for this action.
