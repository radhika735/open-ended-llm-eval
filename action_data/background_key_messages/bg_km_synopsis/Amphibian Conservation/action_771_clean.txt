771: Create walls or barriers to exclude pollutants
Effectiveness rating: Unknown effectiveness
Background information and definitions:
In some situations it may be possible to install barriers to prevent pollutants reaching habitat that supports amphibians.
 

Key Messages:

One controlled study in Mexico found that installing filters across canals to improve water quality and exclude fish increased weight gain in axolotls.
