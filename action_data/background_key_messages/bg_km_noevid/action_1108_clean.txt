1108: Skunk cabbage:  Environmental control (e.g. shading, or promotion of native plants)
Effectiveness rating: No evidence found (no assessment)
Background information and definitions:
Environmental control which includes alterations to shade, and promotion of competitive native plants, may help to control spread of skunk cabbage Lysichiton americanus.
