1145: Water primrose: Use of a tarpaulin
Effectiveness rating: No evidence found (no assessment)
Background information and definitions:
Use of a tarpaulin may provide a mechanism for control of small localised populations of water primrose Ludwigia spp.
 

Key Messages:

No evidence was captured on the use of tarpaulin for control of water primrose.
